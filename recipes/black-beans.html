Black Beans

Description

The beans have layers of chili flavor, balanced with zesty orange, lime, and cilantro. They are spicy, but you can control the level to your taste, depending on which and how much chili to include.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black beans
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp onion powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Soak black beans in water for 1 hour.
  2. Mix black beans, water, olive oil, garlic powder, chili powder, paprika, cumin, salt, onion powder, and black pepper in a bowl.
  3. Heat up some oil in a frying pan. Fry black beans in hot oil until golden brown.
  4. Remove from oil and serve with chili sauce.
